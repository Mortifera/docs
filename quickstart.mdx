---
title: Quickstart
description: Get your first AI-generated blog article in minutes
---

<Note>EdgeBlog is currently invite-only. If you see a "Needs Invitation" page after signing in, contact your organization admin or reach out to **support@edgeblog.ai** for access. Work email users automatically join their company's organization. Personal email users receive a personal organization.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up & create your account">
    Visit [edgeblog.ai](https://edgeblog.ai) and sign in. If you've been invited, you'll be set up automatically with your organization. New users with a work email will join their company's organization.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your website">
    From the dashboard, click **"+ Add Site"** to launch the onboarding wizard:

    - Enter your website URL
    - EdgeBlog automatically analyzes your site's design and creates a matching blog template (takes about 60 seconds)
    - Set your blog path (e.g., `/blog`)
    - Configure a quick content strategy: target audience, writing tone, and articles per month
    - Review and create your site
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate your first article">
    Navigate to your site's **Pipeline** page and click **"Generate Article"**. The AI will:

    - Research your industry and audience
    - Write an SEO-optimized article
    - Generate a header image
    - Score and refine the content

    This takes about 5-10 minutes. You'll see progress updates in real time.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your blog">
    Choose how to serve your blog on your domain:

    - **CDN routing** (recommended): Proxy requests from your domain to EdgeBlog. See [CDN Setup](/setup/cdn-routing).
    - **Blog widget:** Embed your blog on any page with a script tag. See [Blog Widget](/setup/blog-widget).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Your blog template is generated automatically from your website's design. You can customize it anytime from the [Templates](/customization/templates) page.</Tip>
